year: 2016
medium: digital tool, drawing
Processing, digital print

«Titled» is a continuation of the previous work. The outcome is based on the same drawings generated by code, however this time I gave them to a friend, instead of CaptionBot, in order to name them. The titles he came up with became a challenge for me to draw, using the initial image as a bottom layer. This project, together with «Beyonce Choreography», aims at exploring the limits of interpretation and compares thinking processes of human and code.
