year: 2016

The publication summed up the small personal exhibition which theme was the nature of digital image and thus of digital color and pixels. In order to reveal a screen rather as a tangible object than a mysterious media I created a series of physical objects representing such processes as digital decay, optical illussion of color and traces of unique identity behind different screens.
