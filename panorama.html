Panorama Weekly is four publications, weekly "columns" of a kind. Every week I picked one location and took one panorama photograph. Every publication presents the non-hierarchical body of text that those pictures contained, every single line of text starting with city banners and ending with captions on food packaging. The environments I chose are within the area of Scheveningen, The Hague, and start with my own living room, that to my surprise provided me with quite a long textual panorama.
