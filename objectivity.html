/ in collaboration with Celine Hurka and Rudi van Delden /
Essay on the objectivity and logics in graphic design, designed to become a part of "I Hate Graphic Design History" book that brought together all the essays written by 2nd year graphic design students on different topics. Trying to be as objective as possible, we chose to not choose a specific typeface and instead come up with a system to use more than 700 different ones.
