This publication is about conditions that we either live, used to live, or might have been living under. As it is about predictions, assumptions and law. it consists of 381 “if” statements taken from 20 books and articles that explore the subject of mobility and contemplate on it from different perspectives, whether that’s culture, politics, philosophy or simply one’s personal feelings.
