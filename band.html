/in collaboration with Rebecca Rui and Ies Dietsen/
Shoom is a one-perfomance band that explores the phenomena of performing electronic music live. The main objective was to overcome the strive to make an instrument just an instrument, as compact and efficient as possible, and rethink the ways to interact with it on stage. Our point is that a successful musical performance is never just a matter of good music itself, but a triangular relationship between sound, instrument and the performer, where movement becomes the connecting element inbetween.
