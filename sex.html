The Act is an erotic play written for the Sexy Times, exhibition organized by The Royal Academy of Art at SexyLand, the new society for the arts in Amsterdam.
My idea is based on Slavoj Zizek's thoughts on sexual fantasies, and how the worst thing that can happen to one is if it's comes to life. Playing with the subject of sex in different contexts, I presented the text as a web-based audio-performance, that gives a listener/reader a chance to change the performers in real-time.
