How to Always Win is a publication based on brainstorm sessions held together with my classmates in the Royal Academy. The goal of those meetings was to design a public event, a strategy that would provoke competitive behaviour of participants and at the same time reflect different stages of design process. Each spread of the publication presents a new strategy together with its analisys, and highlights different sides of competition sense.
